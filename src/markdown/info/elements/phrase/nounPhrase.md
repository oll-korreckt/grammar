# Noun Phrases
<!-- +elementInfo -->
<!-- !nounPhrase -->
A noun phrase is simply a single head-noun accompanied by all of its modifiers.
<!-- !nounPhrase -->

## Properties
<!-- +propertySummary -->

### Modifiers
> *His elderly* mother just had her 100th birthday.
<!-- .caption -->
The determiner *his* and the adjective *elderly* both modify *mother*.
